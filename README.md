# prek-systematic-review
A Systematic Review of Methods Used to Evaluate State and Locally Funded Pre-K Programs

This website serves as an appendix for the paper titled "A Systematic Review of Research Designs ssed to Evaluate State and Locally Funded Pre-K Programs" authored by Dr. Anamarie A. Whitaker. This repository contains the full citation list of the 285 reports that passed the eligibility screening for our study. These reports represent all studies that met our inclusion criteria during the coding process and form the basis of the analyses presented in the paper.
